On Wednesday 21nd April, Denmark is starting to ease up on the lock down restrictions. The big shopping centers will be opening again as well as restaurants and bars.

To eat inside at a restaurant you must show a negative covid-19 test carried out within the last 72 hours. However, a covid-19 test is not required to eat and drink outside.

We still highly advise that you take extra care and keep your distance when around a lot of people and remember to bring a mask and hand sanitiser.
